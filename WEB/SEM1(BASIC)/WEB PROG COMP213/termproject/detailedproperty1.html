PROMISED LAND

Oprah Winfrey’s house, named “The Promised Land,” is located in Montecito, CA. Inside the approximately 23,000 sq. ft. Neo-Georgian style mansion, there are six bedrooms, 14 bathrooms, a gourmet kitchen and wine cellar, library, theater, and 10 fireplaces. Interestingly enough, it doesn’t appear that there is a swimming pool in the backyard, which is a very common addition to any California mansion.

LOCATION

Montecito, California

PRICE

$88,000,000
