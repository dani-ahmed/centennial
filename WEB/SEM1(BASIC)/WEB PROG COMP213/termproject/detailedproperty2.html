PLAYBOY MANSION

The most famous party house in the world -- the Playboy Mansion -- was sold in 2016 for $100 million. Hugh Hefner bought the house for $1.1 million. The new owner? Hefner's next door neighbor, Daren Metropoulos, the former co-owner and co-CEO of Pabst Brewing Company and a co-owner of Hostess Brands. As part of the deal, Hefner was allowed to remain in the home for the rest of his life.

LOCATION

Beverly Hills, California

PRICE
