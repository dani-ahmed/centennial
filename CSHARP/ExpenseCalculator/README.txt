WELCOME TO MY EXPENSES CALCULATOR

THIS PROGRAM ADDS EXPENSES AND CALCULATES REMAINING CASH, AND ENTERS INTO A TABLE

CHOOSE YOUR INTERVAL IN LINE 1
ENTER NET INCOME IN LINE 2 (TOTAL EARNINGS)
ENTER FOOD EXPENSES IN LINE 3
ENTER RECREATION EXPENSES IN LINE 4
ENTER TRAVEL EXPENSES IN LINE 5
ENTER BILLS EXPENSES IN LINE 6

PRESS SUBMIT TO VIEW RESULT IN DATAGRID

PRESS LOAD TO LOAD BUDGETS FROM TXT FILE (FORMAT: <INTERVAL> <INCOME> <FOOD> <RECREATION> <TRAVEL> <BILLS>

PRESS DELETE TO DELETE SELECTED ENTRY
